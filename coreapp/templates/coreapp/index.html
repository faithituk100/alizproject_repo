About Our Hotel

Experience unparalleled comfort at Aliz Ambruz Hotel, located at 32 New Birth Avenue, Osongama Estate, Uyo, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. Our hotel offers a serene environment complemented by top-notch amenities, including an outdoor swimming pool, a well-equipped fitness center, complimentary Wi-Fi, and a business center. Guests can indulge in delicious meals at our on-site restaurant or unwind with a drink at the bar/lounge or poolside bar. Our air-conditioned rooms feature kitchens, Tempur-Pedic beds with Egyptian cotton sheets, and flat-screen televisions with satellite programming. Conveniently located within a 10-minute drive of local attractions such as the Ibibio Museum and Ibom Hall, Aliz Ambruz Hotel is your ideal choice for both business and leisure stays in Uyo.
